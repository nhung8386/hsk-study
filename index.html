HSK 1 Grammar Points

Subject + 是 + Object
Basic "to be" sentence structure
Used to state what someone or something is
Examples:
我是老师。(Wǒ shì lǎoshī.) - I am a teacher.
他是学生。(Tā shì xuéshēng.) - He is a student.
Subject + 有 + Object
Possession with 有 (yǒu)
Used to express having or possessing something
Examples:
我有一本书。(Wǒ yǒu yī běn shū.) - I have a book.
他有三个苹果。(Tā yǒu sān gè píngguǒ.) - He has three apples.
